A Flexible Monthly Retainer

Dear Castle Rock Team,

Thank you for considering Nano Pulse Digital as a consulting partner for the Town of Castle Rock.

This proposal outlines a 5-hour monthly consulting retainer at a discounted rate of $100/hour (reduced from our standard $150/hour rate), covering strategic, content, and technical guidance for your Civic Plus website and broader digital marketing efforts.

The structure: 12-month term, fixed monthly hours, with insurance coverage included. Simple, predictable, and tailored to municipal needs.

What's included:

5 dedicated consulting hours each month, applied flexibly across strategy, content, and technical recommendations.

A $1,296/year insurance policy (the same policy referenced in this proposal — available for download) covering the engagement.

What These Hours Cover

Your 5 monthly hours can be applied flexibly across any of the following areas. Use them however best serves the Town that month.

01

Marketing Strategy

General marketing strategy consulting — campaign planning, audience targeting, channel recommendations, and digital marketing direction for Town initiatives.

02

On-Page Content Recommendations

Specific page-by-page content suggestions for the Town's Civic Plus website — what to add, refine, or restructure for clarity, accessibility, and search visibility.

03

Content Writing

Drafting and editing copy for pages, announcements, newsletters, or other Town-facing communications when needed.

04

Technical Recommendations

Suggestions and small HTML asset work for the Civic Plus site — buttons, embeds, callouts, layout tweaks, and other lightweight code-level recommendations.

05

Schema & SEO Updates

Structured data (schema markup), metadata optimization, and on-page SEO improvements to help residents and visitors find what they need on search engines.

06

Travel Time

Travel to and from the Town offices for in-person meetings, working sessions, or training counts toward the monthly hour allotment.

What this retainer does NOT include: This retainer is strictly hourly consulting and lightweight recommendations. It does not include website builds, large-scale development projects, custom application work, or major redesigns. Work outside the scope of these hours will be quoted separately and is not part of this agreement.

Insurance Coverage

As required by the Town, Nano Pulse Digital carries an active insurance policy covering this consulting engagement. The full policy document is available below for review by the Town's legal and risk management teams.

Terms & Conditions

A plain-language summary of the agreement. The Town's legal team is expected to review and revise these terms; final language will be negotiated in good faith.

Summary

The Town of Castle Rock ("the Town") is engaging Nano Pulse Digital ("the Consultant") to provide consulting services on a monthly retainer basis. The Consultant will provide 5 hours of consulting work per month at the rates and on the terms outlined in this proposal.

Both parties agree to communicate professionally, provide necessary materials and access in a timely manner, and uphold the obligations described herein. The Consultant will maintain confidentiality regarding any non-public Town information shared during the course of the engagement.

Term

This agreement is for a fixed term of 12 months beginning on the effective date agreed upon at signing. Either party may terminate the agreement earlier with 30 days written notice, subject to payment of fees for services already rendered.

Scope of Services

The Consultant will provide up to 5 hours of consulting services per calendar month. These hours may be applied flexibly across: marketing strategy consulting, on-page content recommendations for the Town's Civic Plus website, content writing, technical recommendations for HTML assets and lightweight code-level work, schema and SEO improvements, and travel time to and from Town offices for in-person work.

This agreement does not include: website builds, major redesigns, custom application development, or other large-scale project work. Such work, if requested, will be scoped and quoted under a separate agreement.

Monthly Hour Allotment

Each calendar month begins with a fresh allotment of 5 consulting hours. Any unused hours expire at the end of the calendar month in which they were allotted and do not roll over into subsequent months. Hours are billed at the discounted rate of $100/hour as part of the flat monthly retainer fee.

Insurance

The Consultant maintains an active insurance policy for the duration of this engagement, as required by the Town. The annual policy cost of $1,296 is amortized across the 12-month term and included in the monthly retainer fee. The full policy document is available for the Town's review at the link provided in this proposal.

Payments

The monthly retainer fee of $608 ($500 consulting + $108 insurance) is invoiced at the beginning of each month and due within 30 days of invoice date. Late balances may accrue interest at 1.5% per month or the maximum rate permitted by Colorado law, whichever is lower.

Out-of-Scope Work

If work requested by the Town exceeds the monthly 5-hour allotment, or falls outside the scope described above, the Consultant will provide a written estimate before proceeding. No out-of-scope work will be performed without prior written approval from the Town.

Confidentiality

The Consultant agrees to maintain the confidentiality of any non-public Town information, records, or communications received during the course of the engagement, both during and after the term of this agreement.

Independent Contractor

The Consultant operates as an independent contractor and is not an employee, agent, or representative of the Town. The Consultant is responsible for their own taxes, benefits, and business expenses.

Ownership of Deliverables

All written content, recommendations, code snippets, schema markup, and other deliverables created specifically for the Town during the engagement are the property of the Town upon payment. The Consultant retains the right to reference the engagement (in general terms) for portfolio and marketing purposes.

Legal Review

The Town's legal team will review and may revise these terms prior to execution. The Consultant agrees to negotiate final contract language in good faith. This document serves as the proposed framework for the engagement.

Governing Law

This agreement will be governed by the laws of the State of Colorado. Any disputes will be resolved in the courts of the appropriate jurisdiction in Colorado.

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if we don't use all 5 hours in a given month?

Unused hours expire at the end of each calendar month and do not roll over. This keeps billing predictable and ensures the retainer reflects current month-to-month value. If the Town consistently underutilizes the hours, we can revisit the structure at renewal.

What if we need more than 5 hours in a particular month?

Additional hours beyond the 5-hour monthly allotment are available at the standard $150/hour rate, quoted and approved in writing before any work begins. We'll never exceed the monthly hours without prior written approval from the Town.

Does travel time count against the 5 hours?

Yes. Travel to and from Town offices for in-person meetings or working sessions is counted toward the monthly hour allotment. We'll always confirm in advance whether a meeting will be in-person or remote so the Town can plan accordingly.

What does the insurance policy cover?

The active policy covers the consulting engagement as required by the Town's contracting requirements. The full policy document is available for download on this page and is included so that the Town's legal and risk management teams can verify coverage details.

Can the Town request a larger project (like a website build) under this agreement?

No. This retainer is specifically for hourly consulting and lightweight recommendations. Larger projects — full website builds, major redesigns, custom application development — would be scoped and quoted under a separate agreement, not drawn from these monthly hours.

Will Castle Rock's legal team be able to revise these terms?

Yes. This proposal is intended as a starting framework. The Town's legal and procurement teams are welcome to review, mark up, and propose revisions. We'll negotiate final contract language in good faith before execution.

Who at Nano Pulse Digital will the Town work with?

You'll work directly with Sean Pooley. Nano Pulse Digital is a one-person operation, which means consistent communication, no account-manager handoffs, and direct accountability for every hour billed.
